How Stockfish Works

Stockfish uses advanced algorithms and heuristics to analyze millions of potential moves. It evaluates positions using a blend of human strategies and machine learning principles.

At its core, Stockfish relies on minimax trees and alpha-beta pruning to process the game state and predict optimal outcomes. Explore its inner workings to understand its brilliance.
